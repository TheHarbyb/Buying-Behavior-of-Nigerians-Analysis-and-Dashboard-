This project explores the buying behaviour of Nigerians using survey-based data.

Transfers are the most preferred payment method, showing a shift from cash-heavy transactions.

WhatsApp, Jumia, and physical stores are the dominant shopping platforms.

Ads, discounts, and product quality are major factors influencing purchases.
